Visualization 1: What are the important factors affecting new technology purchases?

This heat map shows the importance of factors impacting new technology purchases, based on user type.

Respondents (self-classified as ultra nerd, technically savvy, average user, luddite, or undescribed) were asked the importance of various factors (price, features, safety, security, privacy, reliability, user reviews, expert recommendations, convenience, friend/family recommendations) when assessing a new 'tech toy' to purchase, such as a smart TV or a smartphone. Based on the responses, each factor (columns as labeled on the x-axis) was given a score of weighted importance by user class (rows as labeled on the y-axis).

This choropleth map shows the intensity of color corresponding to a score between 0 (least saturation) and 1 (most saturation).
